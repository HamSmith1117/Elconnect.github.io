Federal Maps

Federal Maps are considered to be larger maps that contain multiple States in a single map. There are 3 types of maps.

Clean: State Lines, State Names, and Place Names
Networking: Additional Main Roads and Tracks
Regional: State Lines, State Names, Place Names, and Highlighted Regions

Description

Map 1 also known as Spawn Map, is where the most influential and developed states are located.

States like Elstreet, Maine, Catskills, Oregon, Mt. State, Redmond, Columbia, End State, and the northern half of Green State.

Spawn Map is also home to the Blue, Red, and Yellow regions.

Given that this is where spawn is located, there is a lot of history in the area. Like the development of Elstreet City, the origins of the Government in Mt. City, and major infrastructure projects like Track 1 and the Trail System. It's tough to argue that Spawn is where everything is at.
