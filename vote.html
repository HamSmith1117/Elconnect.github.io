Instructions and Info

This is an html form that is being used as a voting ballot. In the form, in order for your vote to be valid, you must follow theses steps.

  1. Enter your Elstreet ID Number which is given to every citizen of Elstreet.

  2. Enter you gamertag, preferably the same gamertag used in your Elstreet ID.

  3. Select the options available. Depending on the vote, you may be selecting from a list of candidates, or you may be choosing "yes" or "no" on a topic / bill.

  4. Lastly, click the submit button, and once the data in the form has been cleared, click the return button to go back to the home page of the website.

The information that you submit will go to a google sheet that is run by the Elstreet Federal Government and at the end of the voting period, votes will be validated and analize for the results.
