IV Citizenship Act

Proposed: May 1, 2020
Singed: May 25, 2020
Revised: July 25, 2020



Section 1: Beginners Benefits

As a new player, you may depending on State and Federal Laws, be granted your first home or land property for free. You might also be granted a Beginners Package, that will contain nine blocks of Emeralds and Diamonds.
        The Beginners Package may be provided through the Federal Government and its Reserves

Section 2: Becomes A Citizen

After a week of joining, if the player has been active, they may receive their ID and become a Citizen of the Union of Elstreet, and a citizen of the State where they reside. Once a citizen, they will be required to follow all Federal and State legislations, and be expected to pay taxes, and potentially serve in the Military and or in a Jury. They will also receive the privilege to vote in elections.

Section 3: Loss of Citizenship

A citizenship may be revoked if the person is forever kicked from the world, or if the person has become inactive.
        An inactive player shall be someone that has not joined the world in over 3 months and or has not been active in any Elstreet based communities for 3 months.
        If someone that was once active no longer has Minecraft Bedrock Edition, the person has one month to renown the game before they are considered inactive.
        Before citizenship is revoked, it shall be required that the Federal Government must give a 3 day notice, and if there is no response during those 3 days then the citizenship shall be revoked.

Section 4: Inactive Players

Once the citizenship has been revoked all possessions once owned may be given to the State of where the person was living in, or given to the Federal Government if that person did not live in a State.
        Depending on the Persons commitments to the World, they may not have their citizenship revoked even during an absence.
        Cases of peoples activity shall be done by a case by case situation.
