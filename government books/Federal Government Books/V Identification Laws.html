V Identification Laws

Proposed: May 1, 2020
Singed: May 25, 2020



Section 1

An ID allows governments and businesses to know who a person is. It shall also be used as certification of citizenship.
        Once someone has an ID, they are fully protected by the Constitution and the Bill of Rights. And shall be able to participate in citizenship activities.

Section 2

Throughout the several States there shall be ID offices that will manage all IDs within their designated areas.
        People working in these ID looks may be hired by the State or the Federal Government, and will be viewed as Federal Employees.
        If an ID is lost, needs to be updated or needs to be created, or any other related subjects, then the person may go to their local ID office.
        All IDs must be signed by either the Host or one of the CO Hosts in order to become a finished and valid ID.

Section 3

Stealing, and or using someone else’s ID will be viewed is highly illegal with a possibility of a band or kick from the world.
        An ID that is not signed by any of the Host and CO Host, will be viewed as a fake ID and will be highly illegal if proven guilty of using one, with a punishment of a band or kick from the world.
        If a person’s gamer tag, job profession (if their ID states job profession), ID number, and or if the person's primary home location needs to be changed. Then that person will have up to a week to change their ID. After that week the ID will be viewed as invalid and illegal to use.
        Using an invalid ID shall have less of a harsh punishment than using a fake ID or stolen ID, as long as that person was the original owner of the said invalid ID.
