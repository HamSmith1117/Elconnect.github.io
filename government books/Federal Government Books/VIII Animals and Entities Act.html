VIII Animals and Entities Act

Proposed: May 1, 2020
Singed: May 25, 2020



Section 1: Mob Definitions

In order to prevent lag, people may only own a certain number of Pets, Farm Animals, and entities in a State or Federal District.
        Pets may be defined as any mob in the game that can be tamed. Farm Animals may be defined as mobs that can be bread or any other mob that is purposely being kept around.

Section 2: Mob Limits

The Federal maximum amount of Pets and Farm animals shall be up to 16 Pets and 32 Farm Animals.
        States may have the power to make their own Rules and regulation based on Mob limits as long as the number do not exceed 100.

Section 3: Entities

Entities may include but is not limited to, placed entities like any type of Minecarts and Boats, Armor Stands, Paintings, Signs, Item Frames, End Crystals, thrown Tridents, knotted Leads, Banners, Mob Heads, Beacon, Conduit, Bells, and campfires.

Section 4: Entity Limits

The Federal maximum amount of each item shall be 32 with the exemption of items like Mine Carts, Boats and Armor Stands which shall be 16, and Beacon, Conduit, Bells, campfires, which shall be 8 within a 32 blocks radius of each other.
        When it comes to Entities like any type of Chest, Barrels, any type of Furnace, Hopers, Droppers, Dispensers, Cauldron, or any other type of Storage based block. The Federal maximum amount of each item shall be 32 with the exemption of any type of chest or Barrels which may be up to 64.
        States may have the power to make their own Rules and regulation bases on Entities as long as the numbers do not exceed 100.

Section 5: Exemption

Government buildings, Business, and any public or community based buildings will not be counted to an individual but each location will contain their own set Mob and Entity limits.
        Farming Areas may also get their own Mob and Entity limits depending on the law.

Section 6: Punishments

Punishment of barking the State or Federal laws will vary depending on the State and what they decide, but the punishments may include a fine, and or removal of some or all of the Mobs and Entities.
