III Currency Act

Proposed: May 1, 2020
Singed: May 25, 2020



Section 1: Currency and its Value

Emeralds shall be the official form of currency in the Union of Elstreet, and shall be the standard value of all materials, goods, and services.
        The value of currency compared to other material like Diamonds,Gold, and Iron, and the value of interest rates shall be determined by the Federal Reserve Committee

Section 2: Usage

Emeralds shall be used in all aspects in the Federal Government and in the Military. Emeralds shall be used for paying debts, chargers, taxes, and dues.
        Private business or organization are not forced to accept Emeralds to pay for goods and services unless a State law says otherwise. Transactions within a party are also not forced to use Emeralds as payment if all members accept the otherwise payment.

Section 3: Taxation

Both the Federal and State Government may have the power to create their own tax. Taxes must be paid by all people and must be done in Emeralds.
        Implementing and discontinuing a Federal Tax shall require the approval of 80% of seating members in the House.
        If a person will be away from the world then they may either pay the tax in advance or pay the taxes they miss when they get back.
        If an active player doesn’t pay their taxes, and or doesn’t pay the right amount of tax, then they are guilty of tax fraud.
        Punishment of Tax Fraud may be a large fine, loss of property or and temporary banned
        Any person paying a non sale tax must present their ID when paying.
        States may have the power to create their own State run taxes as long as their taxes do not, interfere or go against the Federal Government.

Section 4: Duplication

The House shall have power to make rules and regulations regarding depletion, with the requirement of 80% approval of seating members in the House.
        Any large shortage of duplicated items may only be done in Nether.
        States may be able to make their own rules and regulations on duplication as long as the rules do not interfere or go against the Federal Government.

Section 5: Banks

The House may be able to create a Federal Reserve that may handle their banking and economic needs. States may also be able to make their own State run banks that may have close communication with the Federal Reserve.

Section 6: Committee of the Federal Reserve

There shall be a department of the Federal Reserve which may have the power to run the federal bank and reserves. Every 6 months the President must appoint a committee that will run the Federal Reserve. These people will then be voted in by The House.
        The Committee’s goal shall be to execute the orders on spending for the Legislative and Executive branch, and keep the Federal Reserve and value of the Currency running smoothly.
        The Committee may from time to time when asked to, provide information on the Federal Reserve, and the state of the economy.
        This committee will make decisions based on a voting decision.

Section 7: The Federal Reserves

There will be 3 types of Federal Reserves, the Primary, Secondary, and Host Reserve.
        The Primary Reserve will exist only in the nether, and shall contain any currency and or any items that the government needs. The Primary Reserve shall be the location of where the government makes its currency and items. Only the committee, President, and Hosts may have access to the reserve.
        The Secondary Reserve will be located in a Federal bank, and will only be used as a quick access to some needed items and or currency. All resources in the Secondary Reserve shall be provided by the Primary Reserve. Any member of the House and or specific government officials may have access to this reserve.
        The Host Reserve will only be located in the nether, and its specific location and access to the reserve will only be given to the Hosts. The reserve may only contain currency, essential needs, and any rare items that need to be protected and preserved.

Section 8: Bill Making

Any rules or regulations on regards to Taxes, Duplicating, or any other economic matter, shall contain an expression date to the bill, in order to keep all concurrent documents relevant and can be revisited and reviewed in the future.
